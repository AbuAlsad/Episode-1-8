Structure and Surface Saturn is a gas giant like Jupiter. It is made mostly of hydrogen and helium. Saturn has a thick atmosphere. Saturn has a lovely set of seven main rings with spaces between them.
Time on Saturn One day on Saturn goes by in just 10.7 hours. One year on Saturn is the same as 29 Earth years.
